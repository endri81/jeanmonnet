### About serie of seminars

Each academic year during the first semester, Prof. Endri Raço has organized the seminar cycle "TSS concept and role of Big Data inside European Statistical System: Models and applications in Albanian context". These sessions have provided students with a solid understanding of the core principles of Trusted Smart Statistics.
